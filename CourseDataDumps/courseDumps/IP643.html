IP 643 - Topics in Global Governance
This course examines some of the key policy debates in global governance. Possible topics include global health challenges such as infectious and non-infectious disease management, cities in the global political economy, humanitarianism in war and social crisis, local and global food security, and public and private approaches to challenges of the global commons.

Credits: 0.50
Lecture hours: 3.00

Academic level: Graduate

Faculty: Intl Policy & Governance
Department: *Department Not Declared
