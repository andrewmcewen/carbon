BI 346 - Advanced Molecular Biology
A lecture course covering topics in modern molecular biology. Topics may include the structure of prokaryotic and eukaryotic genes and genomes, concepts of DNA replication, mechanisms and regulation of gene expression, DNA repair and recombination, regulatory RNAs and epigenetics.

Prerequisites: BI226 and BI236 or CH354 and CH356.
Exclusions: BI340, CH358.
Notes: 3 lecture hours

Credits: 0.50
Lecture hours: 3.00

Academic level: Undergraduate

Faculty: Faculty of Science
Department: Biology
